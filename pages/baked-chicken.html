Easy Baked Chicken Thighs

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 4 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 teaspoons onion flakes

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

Step2

Place chicken thighs in a baking dish; season both sides with garlic powder and onion flakes.

Step 3

Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink at the bone and juices run clear, about 30 minutes.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into thickest part of thigh, near the bone, should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 30 mins

Total Time: 35 mins

Servings: 4

Nutrition Facts

162 Calories

8g Fat

3g Carbs

20g Protein
